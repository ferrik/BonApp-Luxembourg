## Important disclaimer
This is not legal advice.
Before public launch or paid partnerships, review the legal pages with a qualified professional if possible.

## MVP legal positioning
BonApp is a directory / discovery platform.

BonApp does not:
- prepare food;
- deliver food;
- process orders;
- process payments;
- issue refunds;
- guarantee restaurant availability;
- guarantee prices or delivery times.

All orders and transactions happen directly between users and restaurants.

## Privacy / GDPR MVP principle
Collect minimum data:
- click events;
- restaurant_id;
- event_type;
- timestamp;
- technical server logs.

Do not collect:
- user names;
- delivery addresses;
- payment details;
- account profiles.
