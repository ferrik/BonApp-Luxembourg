# Skill: Legal Pages MVP

Use this skill when creating or editing Terms of Service, Privacy Policy, Legal Notice, Partner Terms, or footer disclaimers.

## Important
This is not legal advice.
Create minimal MVP legal text only.

## Positioning
BonApp is a directory / discovery platform.
BonApp does not handle orders, payments, delivery, food preparation, refunds, or disputes.

## Required pages
- Terms of Service
- Privacy Policy
- Legal Notice
- Partner Terms

## Language
- English
- French

## Privacy principle
Collect minimum data only:
- click events;
- technical logs;
- no names;
- no addresses;
- no payments;
- no accounts.

## Do not
- invent company registration data;
- use fake legal address;
- add complex cookie consent unless technically required;
- claim GDPR compliance beyond what is implemented.
