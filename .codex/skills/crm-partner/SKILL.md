# Skill: Partner CRM

Use this skill when working with restaurant partnership status, outreach tracking, notes, and future monetization readiness.

## Fields
- partner_status
- notes
- billing_enabled
- pricing_plan

## Partner statuses
- new
- contacted
- interested
- follow_up
- trial
- active
- premium
- paused
- onboarded
- rejected

## Rules
- Keep CRM simple.
- No authentication in MVP unless explicitly requested.
- Do not create a complex sales system.
- Do not implement billing or payments now.
